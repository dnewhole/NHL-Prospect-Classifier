Bigger goaler with upside who saw nine games as back-up in Mississauga, and has assumed a starting role. Sound positionally, excellent reflexes, and glove. Has showed strong play on a team that isn't an elite one. Has excellent anticipation and lightning fast quickness, good rebound control, and has displayed elite level prospect skills and a bright developmental future.   He was selected to the roster for the World Junior Under-18 Tournament in April.
